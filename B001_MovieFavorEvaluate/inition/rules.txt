Preference rules for automatic taste_profile generation:

Positive anchors:
- Movies in my_pos_movies.json are trusted 100-point anchors.
- Fantasy, large-scale sci-fi, disaster, magic, and large action movies can receive a natural bonus when they are satisfying and well executed.
- True stories, biographies, historical adaptations, and documentary-like historical stories receive a natural bonus.
- Stories about resilience, courage, human dignity, freedom, professional faith, or deep emotional relationship analysis receive a natural bonus.
- Directors already appearing in positive anchors or high-score feedback can receive a moderate auxiliary bonus.

Negative anchors:
- Movies in my_neg_movies.json are below 50-point anchors and contain clear rejection reasons.
- Horror, pure commercial formula movies, puzzle-like suspense, war spectacle, and abstract art films should receive moderate penalties unless they have strong human depth, historical texture, or emotional grounding.
- Pure formal experiments, cold anti-narrative structures, forced sentimentality, issue-first persuasion, and mechanism-first suspense are risk signals.

Scoring defaults:
- Positive pass threshold: 80.
- Negative pass threshold: 65.
- Regression target pass rate: 90% for both groups.
- Use low Ollama temperature for repeatable regression tests.
